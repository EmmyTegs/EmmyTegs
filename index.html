About Me

I’m Emmanuel Tega Agbragu, a cybersecurity professional and Master’s student at Carnegie Mellon University Africa, specializing in offensive security and red teaming. With over two years of professional experience in enterprise cybersecurity and hands-on expertise in penetration testing, vulnerability research, and firewall management, I bring deep technical capability combined with strategic thinking.

My recent work includes a large-scale vulnerability assessment on e-government platforms across 55 countries, identifying over 400 confirmed security issues—150 of which were high-severity, such as SQL injection and XSS flaws. My mission is to help organizations think like attackers to defend better.
